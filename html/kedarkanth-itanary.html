Trek Name: Kedarkantha
Early Bird Cost: ₹5,500 (Dehradun to Dehradun) / ₹4,500 (Sankri to Sankri)

Actual Price before Early bird dsliscount: ₹6,900 (Dehradun to Dehradun) / ₹5,500 (Sankri to Sankri) .

Region: Sankri, Uttarakhand
Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights
Grade: Easy to Moderate
Max Altitude: 12,500 ft
Trekking Distance: ~19 km

🔹 Brief Itinerary:

Day 1: Dehradun to Sankri (10-11 hrs drive)
Day 2: Sankri to Juda Ka Talab
Day 3: Juda Ka Talab to Kedarkantha Base
Day 4: Kedarkantha Base to Peak; descend to Juda Ka Talab
Day 5: Juda Ka Talab to Sankri, and back to Dehradun
