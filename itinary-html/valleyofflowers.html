ITINERARY

Hello, Adventurer!

🌄 Day 1: Haridwar - Joshimath 🏞️ Altitude: 6,300 ft
Everyone gathers at Haridwar Railway Station for an uphill drive through scenic sights like Devaprayag, where the Bhagirathi and Alaknanda Rivers merge to form the Ganges 🌊. After reaching Joshimath, enjoy acclimatization activities, a hearty dinner 🍛, and rest 🛏️.

🚶‍♂️ Day 2: Trek from Govindghat to Ghangaria 🌳 Altitude: 6,300 ft to 9,600 ft
Begin the trek to Ghangaria 🌿 with a 14 km gradual ascent after a Himalayan sunrise 🌅 and a sumptuous breakfast 🥞. Explore the quaint hamlet of Ghangaria, enjoy fitness activities 💪, and prepare for the next day's adventure.

🌺 Day 3: Trek from Ghangaria to Valley Of Flowers and back 🌸 Altitude: 9,600 ft to 11,500 ft
Trek to the breathtaking Valley of Flowers 🌼, a magical meadow filled with vibrant blossoms, colorful butterflies 🦋, and serene views 🏔️. Spend time meditating 🧘, walking through nature 🚶‍♀️, or capturing the beauty 📸 before returning to Ghangaria for dinner and games 🎮.

🕌 Day 4: Trek from Ghangaria to Hemkund Sahib ❄️ Altitude: 9,600 ft to 14,107 ft
Trek to the sacred Hemkund Sahib Gurudwara, encircled by seven snow-capped peaks 🏔️. Spend peaceful moments 🕊️, enjoy lunch 🍴, and descend back to Ghangaria for dinner and rest.

🏕️ Day 5: Trek from Ghangaria to Joshimath 🌄 Altitude: 9,600 ft to 6,300 ft
Retrace the route to Govindghat 🛤️ with an easy descent. Optionally, tour Auli in the evening before resting in Joshimath.

🚐 Day 6: Drive from Joshimath to Haridwar 🌟
Bid farewell 👋 as the journey concludes, leaving early from Govindghat to Haridwar for onward travel 🚄. The Valley of Flowers trek ends, leaving memories for a lifetime 🌈.
