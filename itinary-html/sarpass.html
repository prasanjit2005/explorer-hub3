ITINERARY

Hello, Adventurer!

**Day 1: Kasol to Grahan village**
🌄 **Altitude**: 1,700 m to 2,350 m 🏔️
📏 **Distance**: 10 km 🚶‍♂️
🕒 **Time Taken**: 4-5 hours ⏳
🌲 Follow the marked trail through forests along the Grahan Nalah 🌊. Look for makeshift stalls selling tea ☕, omelets 🍳, and rhododendron syrup 🌺. Climb uphill ⬆️ and arrive at Grahan Village 🏘️ with accommodation in guest houses 🏠 or camping grounds ⛺.

📍 **Day 2: Grahan to Min Thach**
🌄 **Altitude**: 2,350 m to 3,400 m 🏔️
📏 **Distance**: 7 km 🚶‍♂️
🕒 **Time Taken**: 4-5 hours ⏳
🌲 Trail north from Grahan climbs gently, passing through dense forests 🌳. Reach Min Thach, a ridge offering views of Chanderkhani and nearby ranges 🏞️. Set up camp ⛺ near a seasonal vendor hut and potable water tap 🚰.

📍 **Day 3: Min Thach to Nagaru**
🌄 **Altitude**: 3,400 m to 3,800 m 🏔️
📏 **Distance**: 8 km 🚶‍♂️
🕒 **Time Taken**: 4-6 hours ⏳
🌲 Start early to avoid slippery snow ❄️. Traverse a steep ridge with breathtaking views 🌄. The tree line ends, giving way to grassy patches and shrubs 🌱. Exercise caution in snow, relying on trekking poles and guides 🧗‍♂️.

📍 **Day 4: Nagaru to Sar Pass and Biskeri Thach**
🌄 **Altitude**: 3,800 m to 4,220 m to 3,500 m 🏔️
📏 **Distance**: 14 km 🚶‍♂️
🕒 **Time Taken**: 10-11 hours ⏳
🌞 Start early to cross Sar Pass before snow softens 🌨️. Follow the ridge-line markers 🧭, keeping left of the steep areas. After crossing, descend to Biskeri Thach and camp near the forest guest house 🌲⛺.

📍 **Day 5: Biskeri Thach to Barshaini**
🌄 **Altitude**: 3,500 m to 2,500 m 🏔️
📏 **Distance**: 12 km 🚶‍♂️
🕒 **Time Taken**: 6-7 hours ⏳
🌲 Follow the trail through forests 🌳 and streams. Pass Pulga village, with its temple and waterfall 💧🏞️. Continue along the Parvati River 🌊, through Nakthan to Barshaini. Return to Kasol 🚐.
