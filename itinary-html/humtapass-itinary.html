ITINERARY

Hello, Adventurer!

Day 1: Driving from Manali to Jobra and Trekking from Jobra to Chika
A Mesmerizing Walk through Lush Green Forests

Start the day with a delicious breakfast before driving from Manali to Jobra, the base camp for the trek.
The scenic drive takes approximately two hours and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape and winding roads.
Upon arrival at Jobra, you will be warmly welcomed and given a briefing about the journey to Chika.
The trek to Chika is a distance of 2 km and takes approximately three hours to complete.
The trail is surrounded by lush greenery, with the Himalayan mountains providing a stunning backdrop.
The trek is relatively easy and serves as a great introduction to the rest of the journey.
Along the way, you will witness the beautiful River Rani flowing through the meadows, with friendly cattle and lambs dotting the landscape.
You will set up camp for the night by the river, providing a tranquil and scenic setting for a restful night's sleep.

Day 2: Chika Campsite to Balu ka Ghera Campsite
🌄 A Day Surrounded by Alpine Beauty 🌺

🌅 Wake up to beautiful daylight and enjoy a hearty breakfast 🍳.
🚶‍♂️ Head towards Balu ka Ghera, a journey of 5-6 hours ⏳.
🌸 Enjoy the beauty of vibrant rhododendron trees, silver birch, and other alpine foliage 🌳.
🏔️ Encounter an arch of the Himalayas known as The White Range and the towering Dhauladhar peaks ⛰️.
💧 Stop at a refreshing waterfall on the left to refill your water supplies.
🏕️ Arrive at Balu ka Ghera, a stunning patch of flat mountain-land dotted with snow ❄️.
🍲 Enjoy a hot lunch and relax amidst the breathtaking scenic beauty.
🌌 As the day ends, dinner 🍛 is served, and the camp 🏕️ is set up for a cozy night's sleep under the stars.

Day 3: Balu ka Gera to Siagoru Trek via Hampta Pass 🏔️
Breathtaking Views of Snow-Covered Mountains ❄️

🌟 Cross Hampta Pass to reach Siagoru for an audacious and thrilling day!
🌱 The terrain starts as meadow-like and green but gradually becomes rocky and barren.
🚶‍♂️ The uphill journey is steep and grueling, with two ridges separated by a leveled plateau region to climb.
🕒 The final 20 to 30 minutes require determination and tolerance, pushing your limits.
🎉 Feel a sense of euphoria upon reaching the top, having surmounted one of the most delicate portions of the journey.
🏘️ Explore the charming Hampta village and enjoy panoramic views of the Spiti and Lahaul valley below.
🍲 Have a hot lunch to reenergize for the descent to the scenic village of Siagoru.
🏞️ The journey through Siagoru is fairly easy once the steep regions are cleared.
🕗 After eight hours of touring, rest and reflect on the trip with fellow trekkers by the campfire. 🔥

Day 4: Siagoru to Chatru, Driving to Chandratal, and Returning to Chatru
🌟 Confluence of Three Passes 🌟

🌄 Start your day with a hearty breakfast and begin the descent from Siagoru to Chhatru.
⏱️ Duration: The journey takes around 2-3 hours and is fairly easy.
🌲 Pass through the breathtaking Hampta Valley, which offers stunning views of the Pir Panjal range of the Himalayas 🏔️.
🏘️ Chhatru: A sparsely populated area at an altitude of 100 bases, it serves as the capstone of three major adventure points in Himachal - Hampta Pass, Spiti, and Rohtang Pass.
🍴 After a delicious lunch, take a scenic 30 km drive to Chandratal Lake 🚗.
Chandratal Lake 🌙 ✨ A scenic gem of Himachal Pradesh: Named after its crescent moon shape, this lake is a must-visit destination.
🌊 The crystal-clear waters reflect the grandeur of the surrounding Himalayas, creating an unforgettable sight.
🏊‍♂️ Things to do: Swim in the pristine waters, take a peaceful walk around the lake 🚶‍♂️, or simply relax and enjoy the stunning views 🌄.
⛺ Camping: Spend the night near the lake under the star-studded sky ✨.

🌄 Day 5: Chatru - Atal Tunnel - Manali 🌉

Wake up to the serene sounds of the moon-shaped lake 🌙, as your Hampta Pass trekking adventure comes to an end.
Traverse through the famous Atal Tunnel 🚇, the longest single-tube highway tunnel above 10,000 feet 🏔️.
Arrive at Manali 🏡 and disperse for your onward journey 🚶‍♂️, carrying a bag full of cherished memories 👜✨.
