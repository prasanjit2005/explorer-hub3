ITINERARY

Hello, Adventurer!

🚐 Day 1: Rishikesh to Lohajung 🛤️ Distance: 225 km Drive
Begin your Brahmatal trek adventure from Rishikesh! Gather at the railway station or bus stand early morning for a scenic 9-hour drive 🚌 passing through Devprayag, Karnprayag, and Rudraprayag 🏞️. Reach Lohajung, a small marketplace in Chamoli district, for dinner 🍛, a trek briefing, and a cozy rest 🛏️.

🌲 Day 2: Lohajung to Bekaltal 🚶‍♂️ Distance: 8 km Trek
Start the trek amidst oak and fir forests 🌳, with mesmerizing views of rhododendrons 🌺 and the Nandaghunti Peak 🏔️. Reach the stunning Bekaltal Lake 🏞️ for a fun night of lakeside camping ⛺. Enjoy activities like snowball fights ❄️, snowman building ⛄, and capturing the beauty 📸.

❄️ Day 3: Bekaltal to Brahmatal 🌅 Distance: 7 km Trek
Wake up to a perfect sunrise 🌄 and trek through snow-covered meadows. Pass the Telindi top and enjoy majestic Himalayan views 🏔️ before reaching Brahmatal or Khabekhal Lake 🌌. Camp here to soak in the serene beauty.

🏔️ Day 4: Brahmatal to Daldum 🌟 Distance: 13 km Trek
Climb to the top of Brahmatal for breathtaking panoramic views of Mt. Trishul, Mt. Nanda Ghunti, and the Panchachuli range 🌠. Descend to the Daldum campsite through enchanting native forests 🌳.

⛰️ Day 5: Daldum to Lohajung 🌄 Distance: 3 km Trek
Return to Lohajung, retracing the trail 🚶‍♀️. Relax in comfortable stay arrangements 🛌, explore the local culture 🛍️, and reflect on the unforgettable journey 🌈.

🌟 Day 6: Lohajung to Rishikesh 🚐
Conclude your trek on a high note as you drive back to Rishikesh 🌅. Explore Rishikesh’s markets and attractions 🛍️ before departing as per your plans ✈️.
